How to Switch to Linux Mint

  1. Download Linux Mint: Visit the Linux Mint download page and download Cinnamon.
  2. Flash it to a USB drive:
    • Download Balena Etcher.
    • Select the Linux Mint ISO, your USB, then click "Flash".
  3. Enter BIOS: Restart and press F2, DEL, or ESC repeatedly.
  4. Boot from USB: Select the USB drive as your boot device.
  5. Try Linux Mint: You can live boot without installing.
  6. Install: Click "Install Linux Mint" on the desktop.
    7. Tada! Congratulations on installing a new revolution of computing
